About

-My name is Zivangie-

You can also call me Ziv or ZG. I am a hobby artist specializing in digital drawing.
I was born in Vietnam, but I live in Germany and grew up there. I love drawing, I loved making up characters as a kid, which is something that hasn't changed much over the years.
I wanna improve my art skills and also share my drawings with ya'll.
Thank you for taking a peek into my world as an hobby artist! I'm excited to continue my exploration of digital art and see where this creative journey takes me next.

Work

-Preface-

Personally, I tend to create my own unique style and original character designs rather than focusing on fan art or anime illustrations. With each digital stroke, I can create something new and invent unique stories.
Currently, my creative energy is in developing and refining my own comic. This has been a cherished aspiration of mine. I've always wanted to weave stories with characters born entirely from my imagination.
I'm also looking forward to turning my artistic visions into tangible products like t-shirts and bags.
